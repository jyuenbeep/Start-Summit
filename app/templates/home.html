Our Goal:

Our mission is to combat the risk of personal privacy being compromised by raising awareness of the dangers associated with data collection by big corporations. We aim to provide insight and resources to our community to minimize the consequences of cybersecurity threats. We strive to better our community through education on the risks associated with data collection practices and potential data breaches by offering statistics, resources, and interactive features throughout our website.
